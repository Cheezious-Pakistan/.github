# Welcome to Cheezious Pakistan Engineering

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for the **Cheezious Pakistan** development and engineering teams. This space is dedicated to building, maintaining, and scaling the technology that powers our brand.

---

## Security & Access Policy

**This is a closed, private organization. Access is strictly limited to authorized Cheezious Pakistan employees, contractors, and approved partners.**

*   **No Public Access:** All source code, projects, and discussions within this organization are strictly confidential. 
*   **Restricted Visibility:** Repository creation is restricted. All new repositories must be created as **Internal** or **Private**. Public repositories are not permitted.
*   **Authorized Personnel Only:** Only organization members with explicitly granted permissions may view, clone, commit, or interact with these repositories. 

## Developer Guidelines

For our internal team members, please adhere to the following organization policies when creating and managing repositories:

1. **Naming Conventions:** Repository names must be exclusively lowercase letters, hyphens (`-`), or underscores (`_`). Numbers and uppercase letters are not allowed.
2. **Access Control:** Do not invite outside collaborators to repositories without approval from the engineering management team.
3. **Branch Protection:** Ensure that the `main` branches of your repositories have proper review requirements enabled before merging pull requests.

## Need Access?

If you are a new team member or an external partner requiring access to specific repositories, please contact your engineering manager or reach out to our internal IT support at **contactus@cheezious.com** with your GitHub username to request an invitation.
